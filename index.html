Hola! I’m Isa, a product designer building products and communities

Originally from Barcelona and living in Switzerland for 7 years, I’m a passionate interface designer that combines an understanding of various fields with a talent for innovative thinking. By creating visionary prototypes and refining products with care, I had the pleasure to work for companies like Disney Research, Constructor or Appfire while winning awards and prizes. Additionally, I’m a proud founder of a thrilling community garden.
